Age comp data, whole catch, Fishery (plot 1 of 3).
'N input' is the input sample size. 'N adj.' is the sample size after adjustment by the Dirichlet-Multinomial Θ parameter based on the formula N adj. = 1 / (1+Θ) + N * Θ / (1+Θ).
For this fleet, Θ = 0.345 and the sample size multiplier is approximately Θ / (1+Θ) = 0.256. For more info, see Thorson et al. (2017).
